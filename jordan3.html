Jordan 3 Sneakers

The Jordan 3, released in 1988, is a groundbreaking sneaker in the Air Jordan line. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, it introduced innovative features like the visible Air cushioning and the iconic elephant print. The Jordan 3 became an instant classic, beloved by sneakerheads and athletes alike, and is considered one of the most influential sneakers in history.

  • High-top silhouette with premium leather and suede materials
  • Visible Air cushioning in the sole for superior comfort
  • Iconic elephant print on the toe and heel panels
  • Distinctive Jumpman logo and "Nike Air" branding on select releases
  • First sneaker worn by Michael Jordan during his first dunk contest win
